Your privacy matters

We are committed to transparent processing, responsible data handling, and clear rights management for every person interacting with our services.

In brief

  • We process personal information to provide services, comply with law, and support legitimate business operations.
  • We do not sell your personal information collected through our website.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limits.

Introduction

This privacy notice explains how we collect and process information that we collect from you, or that you provide to us. It covers information that could identify you ("personal information") and information that could not. In the context of the law and this notice, "process" means collect, store, transfer, use, or otherwise act on information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. Our policy complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Except as set out in this notice, we do not share, sell, or disclose to a third party any information collected through our website.

1. Information Officer

We have appointed an Information Officer responsible for ensuring this policy is followed. For any privacy questions or to exercise your legal rights, contact: info@utc-sa.co.za

2. Information we process

We may collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal information, including:

  • Identity information: first name, last name, title, date of birth, and similar identifiers.
  • Contact information: billing address, delivery address, email address, telephone numbers, and related communication details.
  • Financial information: bank account and payment card details (where applicable).
  • Transaction information: details about payments and services purchased from us.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type/version, time zone, operating system, and device-related technology.
  • Profile information: username, password, service history, interests, preferences, feedback, and survey responses.
  • Marketing information: communication preferences and responses to marketing interactions.

We may also use aggregated anonymous statistical or demographic data that does not directly identify you.

3. Special personal information

We do not intentionally collect special personal information (such as race or ethnicity, religious beliefs, health data, biometric data, trade union membership, political opinions, sex life, or criminal data), unless there is a lawful basis to do so.

4. If you do not provide required personal information

Where we are required by law or contract to collect personal information and you do not provide it, we may be unable to perform that contract or provide services to you. If this applies, we will inform you at the relevant time.

The bases on which we process information about you

5. Contractual necessity

When we enter a contract with you, we process your information to fulfill our obligations, including to:

  • verify your identity for security purposes
  • provide products or services
  • offer practical guidance about our services and website usage

We process this information for as long as the contract remains in force and as required afterward for legal or operational purposes.

6. Consent

Where no contract exists (for example, browsing our website or requesting information), we may process your information based on your consent. Where possible, we seek explicit consent (for example, for cookies).

If you have provided consent for marketing-related sharing with selected associates, we will rely on that consent until withdrawn.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting unsubscribe@utc-sa.co.za.

7. Legitimate interests

We may process information for legitimate interests where it is reasonable and proportionate, including for:

  • record-keeping and business administration
  • responding to communications you send to us
  • protecting legal rights and obtaining professional advice
  • protecting your interests where we believe we have a duty to do so

8. Legal obligations

We may process your information where required by law, including where lawful requests are made by authorities.

Specific uses of information you provide to us

9. Information posted for sharing

If you post information in public or shared areas of our website (where enabled), that information may be read, copied, downloaded, or used by others. Once information enters the public domain, we cannot control third-party use.

Subject to legal requirements, you may request deletion of personal information you have posted by contacting unsubscribe@utc-sa.co.za.

10. Complaints regarding website content

If you complain about content on our website, we will investigate. Where justified or legally required, we may remove content while reviewing the matter.

11. Information relating to your method of payment

Payment information is generally handled by reputable payment service providers and not processed directly by us unless explicitly stated at the point of payment.

12. Information about your direct debit

Where direct debit arrangements apply, relevant information is shared with our bank for processing according to your authorization and our instructions.

13. Job application and employment information

If you submit information for a job application, we may keep it for up to three years for recruitment purposes. Employment records are processed for employment administration and retained according to legal and operational requirements.

14. Communicating with us

When you contact us by phone, email, or website forms, we use the information provided to respond and to maintain service records.

15. Complaints handling

We record complaint details in order to resolve concerns and improve service quality.

16. Affiliate and business partner information

Information received from affiliates or business partners is used to administer referral and partner relationships and related payments.

Automated collection when you visit our website

17. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed by your browser. We use cookies and similar tools to improve functionality, remember preferences, and understand website usage.

On your first visit, we request cookie preferences where required. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some website functionality may be limited.

18. Personally identifiable information from browsing activity

Our servers may record request data such as IP address, location signals, internet service provider, browser and device details. We use this information to monitor performance, security, and service quality.

19. Re-marketing

We may use third-party re-marketing services. Where applicable, this is based on your cookie preferences.

Disclosure and sharing

20. Information from third parties

We may receive limited information from trusted service providers. Where possible, this is not personally identifiable.

21. Third-party advertising

Third-party advertisers may use cookies or similar technologies on their own systems. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy notices.

22. Credit reference

Where necessary to prevent fraud, information may be shared with relevant credit reference entities in line with applicable law.

23. Processing outside South Africa

While our websites are hosted in South Africa, some service providers may process data in other countries. We apply reasonable safeguards where cross-border processing occurs.

Control over your information

24. Duty to inform us of changes

Please keep us informed if your personal information changes so we can maintain accurate records.

25. Access to your personal information

You may request access to personal information we hold about you by contacting us.

26. Removal of your information

You may request deletion of your personally identifiable information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

27. Verification of identity

Before actioning access, correction, or deletion requests, we may verify your identity to protect your information.

Other matters

Our website and services are not directed at children for independent purchase or contracting. Users under 18 should use this website with consent from a parent or guardian.

29. Encryption of data sent between us

We use SSL/TLS certificates to encrypt data transmitted between your browser and our website.

30. How you can complain

If you are unhappy with this privacy notice or have a complaint, please contact us first so we can attempt to resolve the matter in good faith.

31. Retention period for personal data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and support legal claims or defenses.

32. Compliance with law

This notice is intended to align with applicable legal requirements in jurisdictions where we operate.

33. Review of this privacy policy
